State Data Breach Notification Statutes

This website provides general information and is not intended as legal advice

Information

Each state has their own data breach notification statute. More information on each can be found by following the links below. In general states require that an entity notify consumers when their personal data has been compromised. The potential penalties are often fines or legal action. Many have exceptions for data that is encrypted or otherwise protected.
