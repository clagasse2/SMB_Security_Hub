Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation

This website provides general information and is not intended as legal advice


Summary

This regulation applies to DoD contractors and subcontractors

Subject Entities This regulation applies to US Department of Defense (DoD) contractors. It requires contractors and subcontractors that possess, store, or transmit “covered defense information”
Penalty for Non-Compliance Failure to comply may result in debarment of the contract
How to Comply
  • This regulation mandates compliance with a specific cybersecurity standard: the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) Special Publication (SP) 800-171
  • The Regulation provides a detailed process for investigating cyber incidents and reporting them to the DoD and the prime contractor (or next higher-tier subcontractor), including protecting and preserving evidence that includes malware for possible forensic analysis
