Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

This website provides general information and is not intended as legal advice


Summary

This regulation is a privacy and security law related to information of children

Subject Entities COPPA applies to websites and online services that are directed at children under the age of 13. It also applies if the site poerator has actual knowledge that children under the age of 13 are using a website
Penalty for Non-Compliance The Act is enforced by the FTC using fines
How to Comply
  • Provide a reasonable means for a parent to review the personal information collected from a child, and enable them to refuse to permit its further use or maintenance
  • Not make the child’s participation in a game, the offering of a prize, or another activity cannot be a condition for a child to provide information
  • Provide reasonable procedures to protect the confidentiality, security, and integrity of personal information collected from children
More Information 15 U.S. Code Chapter 91 & 16 CFR Part 312
