Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act

This website provides general information and is not intended as legal advice


Summary

This law has security, privacy, and breach notification rules regarding healthcare information

Subject Entities The law applies to health care providers, health plans, health care clearinghouses, and, in some instances, business associates of these businesses called covered entities
Penalty for Non-Compliance Fines depend on the nature and extent of the violation and, the extent to which the organization has attempted to protect information
How to Comply There are a few different parts of HIPAA compliance including
  • Protecting ePHI with administrative, physical, and technical controls
  • The privacy rule has specific requirements for the disclosure of ePHI
  • The breach notification rule requires notification within 60 day along with other notification
More Information 45 CFR Part 160, 45 CFR Part 164
