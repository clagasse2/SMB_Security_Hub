Maryland Information Security Standard

This website provides general information and is not intended as legal advice


Summary

This regulation requires an organization to implement and maintain reasonable security procedures and practices that are appropriate to the nature of the personal information owned or licensed and the nature and size of the business and its operations to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, modification, or disclosure

Subject Entities Applies to all businesses that handle personal information under the statute
Penalty for Non-Compliance
  • Violators may be prosecuted by the Maryland Attorney General, subject to civil suits and civil penalties, or subject to other remedies
  • Violations may also be deemed to be unfair acts or practices under Maryland law
How to Comply This staute doesn't define reasonable security procedures and practices. It is recommended to comply with the Massachusetts Information Security Standard
