Federal Trade Comission Act 5

This website provides general information and is not intended as legal advice


Summary

This regulation requires security and privacy measures

Subject Entities The law applies to almost every organization in the US, except for banks and common carriers
Penalty for Non-Compliance The FTC imposes heavy fines with a $5 billion fine in a recent case concerning Facebook
How to Comply This act requires an organization to engage in "reasonable and necessary" security practices. However these are generally undefined. Compliance with GLBA is a good start
