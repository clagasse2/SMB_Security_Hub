Consumer Privacy Protection Act of 2017

This website provides general information and is not intended as legal advice


Summary

This regulation seeks to ensure the privacy and security of sensitive personal information

Subject Entities This act applies to organizations that collect, use, access, transmit, store, or dispose of sensitive personally identifiable information of 10,000 or more US citizens during any 12-month period
Penalty for Non-Compliance Civil penalty fines will not exceed $5 million unless the violation is found to be willful or intentional, in which an additional $5 million can be imposed
How to Comply
  • Required to implement a comprehensive consumer privacy and data security program
  • Following the discovery of a security breach of sensitive personally identifiable information (PII), a commercial entity must notify an affected U.S. resident and provide identify theft prevention and mitigation services
