Alabama Information Security Standard

This website provides general information and is not intended as legal advice


Summary

This regulation requires an organization to implement and maintain reasonable security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized acquisition

Subject Entities Applies to individuals, commercial entities (including nonprofits), trusts, estates, governmental entities, and their respective third-party agents that acquire or use sensitive personally identifying information. Entities that are subject to federal laws, regulations, or guidance on data breach notification may be excepted from certain provisions if the entity maintains procedures and provides notice to affected individuals consistent with those laws
Penalty for Non-Compliance The Attorney General has exclusive right to bring an action for violation of the statute
How to Comply Implement security measures practicable for the size of the entity. The following must be considered
  • Designating an employee to coordinate the entity's security measures
  • Ientify internal and external risks
  • Adopt safeguards to address the identified risks
  • Utilize third-party providers
  • Evaluate safeguards to account for changes in the entity
  • Keep the entities management informed regarding security
  • Must take reasonable measures to dispose, or arrange for the disposal, of records containing personal information when such records are no longer needed for business purposes or to comply with applicable law or regulations
Exceptions Entities that are subject to federal laws, regulations, or guidance on data breach notification may be excepted from certain provisions if the entity maintains procedures and provides notice to affected individuals consistent with those laws
